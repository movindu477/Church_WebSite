Priests Who Shaped Us

This page serves as a tribute and resource to honor all the priests who have served our parish throughout the years. Through this website, you can search and learn more about the spiritual leaders who have guided our community, celebrated the sacraments among us.

Fathers who Served in the Kattuwa church

This section honors the devoted priests who faithfully served the Kattuwa Church. Their spiritual journey began in service to the parish, where they nurtured the faith of the community, celebrated the sacraments, and lived as shepherds among the people. Eventually, they chose the path of consecrated life — embracing prayer, simplicity, and obedience in pursuit of a closer union with God. We remember their contributions with gratitude and continue to pray for their peace and holiness.

The Sons of Priests born from the Kattuwa Sangha

Rev. Fr. Thomas Parris

Born on 24 June 1949 in Kattuwa and ordained as a priest on 15 November 1980 at St. Anne's Church, Palathuraya. He served as the Senior Father of the Congregation of the 'Nazareth' Presbyterian Fathers, Wennappuwa, and contributed extensively as a teacher and spiritual guide. He passed away peacefully on April 5, 2022.

Rev. Fr. Anthony Basil Gamini Silva (Nirmala Maria Sect)

Also born on 24 June 1949 and ordained on 15 November 1980. He served in several parishes and seminaries including Ampitiya and Nochchiyagama. He contributed significantly to the spiritual education sector until his passing on April 5, 2022.

Rev. Fr. Andrew Shelton Welikadarachi (Colombo Diocese)

Born on 11 May 1955 and ordained on 24 July 1982. He served in Mattakkuliya and was a lecturer and Vice-Rector at the National Theological Seminary after his theological studies in Rome. Currently serves as Dehiwala Meesam Sevaka Father.

Rev. Fr. Rohan Perera (Sect of the Divine Savior)

Born on 30 October 1957, ordained in 1985, and has served in Melsiripura, Kekirawa, and Thimbirigasyaya. After higher education abroad, he became a deacon in several congregations. Currently serves as parish priest in Hewadivela.

Rev. Fr. Dunstan Piyatissa Welikadarachi (Nirmala Maria Nikaya)

Born on 4 March 1964, ordained on 3 August 1991. Served in Solewewa, Modara, Wellawaya, and Hambantota. Educated in Rome and the Philippines, currently serves at Our Lady of the Assumption Church, Maradana.

Rev. Fr. Chanaka Prabath Welikadarachi (Colombo Diocese)

Born on 14 April 1982 and ordained on 20 April 2013. He has served in Nayakakanda, Kalamulla, and Pitipana, and now serves as the Meesam Servant Father of Ekala New Meesam.

Rev. Fr. Asiri Devthilina Costa (Colombo Diocese)

Born on 12 November 1992 and ordained on 6 June 2021. Currently serves as Assistant Father and the YCS Coordinator Father for the East Negombo District.



The Nuns who emerged from the Kattuwa Sangha

Venerable Sister Regina Paris

She was ordained as a nun. Born on 29.12.1913 in Palagathuraya village, Marawila, Sri Lanka, she served as the Mother Superior of the hostel and Wennappuwa monastery on 1935.09.08. She passed away on 27.09.2007. Hon. Milanthi Jayawardena Kana

Venerable Sister Milanthi Jayawardena

Born on 25.41.1978 in Kattuwa, she was ordained as a nun in the Order of the Little Sisters of the Poor on 31.07.2005. She served as a missionary in Australia and France from 2005 to 2022 and is currently serving in India.
