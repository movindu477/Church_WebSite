History Of ST. Francis De Sales Church from 1923 to 1998

The St. Francis De Sales Church in Kattuwa, Negombo, has been a spiritiual hub for over 175 years. The original Land was accuired in 1846, with the current site gifted in 1902 following a miraculous resolution of a land dispute. The present church was built in 1911 under Rev. Fr. Brault, with significant support from Frs. Albaron, Britton, and Nissan. Intially known as the "Stone Church", it was complted through the efforts of Fr. Nissan and the loacl Community. Tradition such as the March Novena and Feast of St. Joseph, the Devamatha Novena, and the flag tree ritual have enriched parish life. Community contributions led to the expansion of facilities including a Mission House, dining hall, and meeting spaces. Rev. Julius Wijesuriya's introduction of Gnan Upadesha classes and a 15-day Dharma retreat in 197o marked a spritual revival, honored by a large Holy Cross Donation. Clergy from the Dblate of Mary Immacolate served until 1976, followed by diocesan preists.

The 75th Jubilee (1998 - 1999) brought major renovations, a new Dhamma School, and spiritual programs. Outreach included publishing a booklet on St. Francis De Sales and distributing Bibles with German mission support. In 2006, the church became independent, with Rev. Fr. Jude Samantha as its first Parish Preists. Further development under Rev. Fr. Dinesh Priyashantha and Fr. Manjula Niroshan included meditation programs, infrastructure improvements, and cultural events. Dedicated clergy, Nuns, and lay leaders have driven education and youth activities. A new Damma School building (1999), fencing, and restored premises enhanced the site, funded by local and international support. Youth Society intiatives, including an annual fair, have helped fund church projects. Under Rev. Fr. Prasad Pannamperuma, community programs like "Breakfast under the Tree" and interactive Dhamma School events revived parish engagement.

The annual feast of St. Francis de Sales Church

The annual feast of St. Francis de Sales Church in Kattuwa, Negombo, is celebrated on January 24th. This date marks the memorial of St. Francis de Sales, a significant figure in the Catholic Church, and is observed by the Kattuwa parish.
